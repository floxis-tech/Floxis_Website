Infrastructure, Not Tools

Floxis provides modular, server-side monetization infrastructure that publishers and ad networks can deploy independently or together — all designed to operate inside existing ad stacks without disrupting control, auctions, or ownership.

Infrastructure Layers

Three Layers of Server-Side Infrastructure

Server-side oRTB Infrastructure

A foundational server-side layer for managing pricing, routing, and traffic behavior across demand. Floxis' oRTB infrastructure enables publishers and ad networks to control how demand is handled and monetized, while preserving existing ad servers, auction logic, and demand relationships.

Learn more

Publisher-owned Prebid.js Adapter Infrastructure

A publisher-operated Prebid.js adapter that works across wrappers and remains fully owned and controlled by the publisher. This infrastructure allows publishers to extend monetization logic beyond the browser, reduce vendor dependency, and operate Prebid.js adapters on their own terms — independent of any single wrapper or demand partner.

Learn more

Server-side Prebid Server Infrastructure

A fully server-side Prebid Server environment designed for scale, efficiency, and enterprise-grade execution. Floxis enables publishers and networks to operate Prebid Server infrastructure that supports high-volume traffic, centralized governance, and low-latency performance in production environments.

Learn more

Interoperability

Designed to Work Together — or Independently

Each Floxis infrastructure layer can be adopted on its own or combined with others, depending on how your monetization stack is structured.

  • Standards-aligned
  • Publisher- and network-controlled

Floxis does not replace core systems — it adds control where it matters.
