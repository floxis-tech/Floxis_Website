Products

Infrastructure, Not Tools

Floxis provides modular, server-side monetization infrastructure that publishers and ad networks can deploy independently or together — all designed to operate inside existing ad stacks without disrupting control, auctions, or ownership.

Infrastructure Layers

Three Layers of Server-Side Infrastructure

Server-side RTB Infrastructure

A foundational server-side layer for managing pricing, routing, and traffic behavior across demand — while preserving existing ad servers, auction logic, and demand relationships.

Learn more

Publisher-owned Prebid.js Adapter Infrastructure

A publisher-operated Prebid.js adapter that works across wrappers and remains fully owned and controlled by the publisher — independent of any single wrapper or demand partner.

Learn more

Server-side Prebid Server Infrastructure

A fully server-side Prebid Server environment designed for scale, efficiency, and enterprise-grade execution in production environments.

Learn more

Interoperability

Designed to Work Together — or Independently

Each Floxis infrastructure layer can be adopted on its own or combined with others, depending on how your monetization stack is structured.

  • Server-side by design
  • Standards-aligned
  • Publisher- and network-controlled
  • Built to integrate with existing ad servers, wrappers, and demand partners
