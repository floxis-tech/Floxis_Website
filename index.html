What the Infrastructure Enables

Floxis provides native server-side controls that let publishers and ad networks manage demand behavior inside their existing ad stack.

Supply Path Control (SPO)

Define and enforce preferred demand paths without interfering with auction logic.

Pricing & Floor Control

Set and manage pricing rules across demand while preserving full auction transparency.

Traffic Shaping & Routing

Shape, segment, and route traffic server-side based on business and performance rules you control.

QPS Management

Controlled Experimentation

Test pricing, routing, and demand strategies safely within your existing stack.
