Prebid.js Infrastructure

Publisher-Owned Prebid.js Infrastructure

Floxis provides publisher-owned Prebid.js infrastructure that allows publishers and ad networks to operate and control their own Prebid.js adapters — independent of wrappers, vendors, or demand partners.

Infrastructure

What Floxis Provides

Floxis delivers the infrastructure required to operate Prebid.js adapters as a first-class, publisher-controlled component of the monetization stack.

  • Publisher-owned Prebid.js adapter infrastructure
  • Server-side endpoints and routing logic
  • Pricing, traffic shaping, and governance controls
  • Operational support for scale and production environments

Wrapper Integration

Wrapper-Agnostic by Design

Floxis' Prebid.js infrastructure is designed to work across wrappers, without creating dependency on any single wrapper or vendor.

The wrapper runs the client-side auction. The publisher owns the Prebid.js adapter and logic.

In this model:

  • The wrapper executes the client-side auction
  • The publisher-owned Prebid.js adapter (enabled by Floxis) routes requests
  • Server-side logic handles pricing, routing, and governance
  • The ad server retains full control over auction outcomes

Capabilities
